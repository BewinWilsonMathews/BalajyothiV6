About ESAF Balajyothi Club

ESAF Balajyothi is a flagship programme of ESAF Foundation, designed to empower rural children aged 6-18 years with essential life skills for holistic development. Supported by ESAF Small Finance Bank’s CSR, the programme focuses on nurturing the intellectual, physical, and emotional growth of children in rural and underserved communities.

Counselling by ESAF Santhwana

Counselling is a journey of guidance, healing, and support that helps individuals face life’s challenges with clarity and strength. It offers a safe space to share burdens, explore emotions, and find constructive ways to move forward. Through counselling, people discover new perspectives, develop resilience, and experience inner peace. 

ESAF Santhwana stands as a compassionate initiative that provides such care and support. True to its name, Santhwana—which means comfort and consolation—it reaches out to those in need, offering professional and empathetic counselling services. By walking alongside individuals in their struggles, ESAF Santhwana seeks to bring hope, healing, and renewed purpose to lives.
